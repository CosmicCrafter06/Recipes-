Chicken Karahi

History

A beloved dish in Pakistan, Chicken Karahi originates from the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa region. Traditionally cooked in a wok-like pan called a "karahi," it is known for its rich, spicy tomato-based gravy

Recipe

  1. Heat ghee and oil in a pan, sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and green chili, followed by chicken and salt. Cook until the chicken changes color.
  3. Stir in tomatoes, coriander, cumin, and red chili powder.
  4. Cover and cook for 20 minutes, then uncover and sauté for 10 minutes.
  5. Add whisked yogurt, black pepper, and garam masala.
  6. Garnish with green chilies, ginger, and cilantro. Serve hot with naan or rice.
