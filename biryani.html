Biryani

History

Biryani was brought to the Indian subcontinent by Persian traders and later refined by Mughal chefs. It is a fragrant rice dish layered with spiced meat and saffron.

Recipe

  1. Marinate meat with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice for an hour.
  2. Cook basmati rice with whole spices until 70% done.
  3. Fry onions, add marinated meat, and cook until tender.
  4. Layer rice and meat, sprinkle saffron water, and cover
  5. Steam for 20 minutes on low heat
  6. Garnish with fried onions and serve with raita.
